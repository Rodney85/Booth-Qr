## 1. The Direct Greeting
**Prompt**:
> Mobile splash screen, **Flat Design** aesthetic. 
> **Background**: Solid Deep Navy (#071426). **Strictly no gradients.**
> **Key Features**: 
> - A centered logo in solid vibrant white. 
> - Text: "Welcome to Precision." (32px Inter Bold, -0.022em tracking, White).
> - One-word subtext: "PROACTIVE" in 11px uppercase +0.08em tracking.
> - A rectangular button with 8px radius, Solid CS-Blue (#0A6AE2).
> - Layout: Mathematically centered, generous whitespace.
